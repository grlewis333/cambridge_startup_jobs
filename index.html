What is this?

A personal project to map Cambridge's tech startup ecosystem and surface job opportunities that don't make it onto LinkedIn or Indeed ‚Äî companies that often hire by direct outreach.

How the data is gathered
1
Hub scraping ‚Äî 19 Cambridge startup hubs were scraped (Cambridge Innovation Capital, deeptech labs, Cambridge Enterprise, St John's Innovation Centre, etc.), producing ~430 companies with websites.
2
Companies House bulk data ‚Äî the full UK company register (5.6M companies) was filtered to active Cambridge-postcode tech companies (SIC codes 62x, 63x, 72x, 21x, 26x, 71x), producing 314 additional companies.
3
Merge & validation ‚Äî hub companies are fuzzy-matched against Companies House records (token Jaccard similarity) to confirm active status and add postcode/SIC data. 332 companies are CH-verified.
4
AI enrichment ‚Äî company descriptions, sector tags, stage estimates, and hiring status are generated by GPT-4o mini from company website content and/or training knowledge. Careers pages are scraped where accessible.
